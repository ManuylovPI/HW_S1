How to get a Hugging Face Token (Optional)

  1. Go to huggingface.co and create a free account
  2. Click on your profile picture and select "Settings"
  3. Go to "Access Tokens" in the left menu
  4. Generate a new token with "read" access (no special permissions needed)
  5. Copy the token and paste it above for faster model downloads
  6. Note: Without a token, the model will still download but may be slower
